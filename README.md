# BMW Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **BMW Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**.
The dashboard helps analyze BMW sales performance across **years, months, regions, countries, models, and sales channels**.
It is designed to provide clear business insights for decision-making.


## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
- DAX (Measures & Calculations)
- Data Visualization & Dashboard Design


## Dataset Information
- Dataset Type: Sample / business-style dataset
- Time Period: **2019 – 2023**
- Data Includes:
  - Year, Month, Week
  - Region & Country
  - BMW Models
  - Sales Quantity
  - Revenue
  - Sales Channel (Online, Dealership, Wholesale)
- Note: Dataset used only for learning and portfolio purposes.


## Dashboard Features
- 📊 Revenue and Quantity Sold analysis by Month, Week, and Year
- 🚗 Top-selling BMW models comparison
- 🌍 Country-wise sales and revenue analysis
- 🧩 Interactive slicers for:
  - Year
  - Region
  - Country
  - Model
  - Sales Channel
- 🏷️ Model-wise detailed view with:
  - Average price
  - Year-wise quantity sold
  - Revenue trends
- 📈 Year-over-Year (YoY) sales comparison
- 🛒 Sales channel contribution analysis


## Key Insights
- BMW X-series models (X5, X4, X3) contribute significantly to overall sales.
- Sales show noticeable variation across years, with strong performance after 2021.
- The United States and European countries generate higher revenue compared to other regions.
- Dealership channel contributes the highest share of total sales quantity.
- Premium models like BMW i8 and BMW X4 have higher average prices.


## Dashboard Pages
1. **Main Dashboard**
   - Overall sales performance
   - Top models
   - Country & channel analysis
2. **All Models View**
   - Individual BMW model analysis
   - Year-wise quantity sold and revenue trends
